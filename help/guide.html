List of chat commands:

The most common commands can be accessed by pressing 'C' to open the quickmenu.

  • Drop money:
    • !dropmoney /dropmoney !dm /dm

  • Drop active weapon:
    • !dropweapon /dropweapon !drop /drop !dw /dw

  • Upgrade structure:
    • !upgrade /upgrade !upg /upg !up /up

  • Buy and sell doors:
    • !buydoor /buydoor !bd /bd !selldoor /selldoor !sd /sd
